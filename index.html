About

My name is Sydney Mai. I write code and do Photoshop. Big time.

Hello - if you're a stranger to this page, my name is Sydney Mai. Nice to meet you ☺ I live in this place called Brooklyn, New York where people eat salad out of mason jars and every apartment has at least one exposed brick wall.

I started writing my first line of code not too long ago but am already obsessed with building beautiful websites. When I'm not coding, you can find me editing photos. Editorial photography is also one of my favorite things.

I'm currently a senior at NYU majoring in Media & Communication with a minor in Web Development.
